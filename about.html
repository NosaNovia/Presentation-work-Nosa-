How did this start?

Teenagers in a circle. A group of us started to reflect on its primes and lows.

Sleepovers. Parties. Gossip. *Drama* Being young was funnnnn. But it was also painful. Exhausting. Full of tears and hurt.

So we talked about it. It was enthralling, deep, hilarious.

We bared our hearts to each other, some of us for the first time.

We were honest and it didn't kill us.

So we thought this could be cool to enable others to do so as well.
